About the Competition

  • Teams of up to 3 (supervisor not included)
  • Submit abstract for one of the 3 tracks
  • Present research to a panel of experts
  • Win prizes and recognition for research!

Competition Themes

Core Computer Sciences

For students working on foundational research in computing.

  • Algorithms & Data Structures
  • Computational Complexity & Automata Theory
  • Machine Learning & AI Theory
  • Programming Languages & Compiler Design
  • Cryptography & Secure Computing
  • High-Performance & Quantum Computing

Applied Computer Science & Interdisciplinary Research

For students exploring AI and computing applications in diverse fields.

  • AI & Data Science for Engineering Solutions
  • Human-Computer Interaction & Smart Interfaces
  • Virtual, Augmented & Mixed Reality (VR/AR/MR)
  • AI for Healthcare, Sustainability & Smart Cities
  • Cyber-Physical Systems & IoT-based Solutions
  • Robotics, Automation & Digital Twins

AI & Emerging Technologies in Other Disciplines

For students from Mechanical, Civil, Electrical, Biotechnology, and other disciplines who apply AI and computational methods to their research.

  • Smart Materials & AI-assisted Manufacturing
  • Structural Health Monitoring & AI in Construction
  • AI-driven Power Systems & Renewable Energy
  • AI for Bioinformatics & Healthcare Innovations
  • Sustainable Transport, Traffic Management & AI-driven Infrastructure
